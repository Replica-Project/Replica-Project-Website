What is Pandabuy

Pandabuy is a Chinese trading company that acts as a middleman between you and the seller. They provide you with a range of services, including purchasing your items, shipping your items to you, and handling customs clearance. They also offer a range of additional services, such as photo reviews and QC (quality control) checks.

Pandabuy is a trusted and reliable company that has been in operation for over 10 years. They have a large network of suppliers, and they offer a wide range of products for sale. They also offer a very user-friendly platform, making it easy to find the products you're looking for and to place an order

When buying through Pandabuy, you will need to create an account and then log in to your account. Once you're logged in, you can start browsing the products available for sale. You can filter your search by category, brand, price, and other factors.

Once you've found the product you're interested in, you can add it to your cart. You can then proceed to checkout and pay for your order. Pandabuy will then purchase the product from the seller and ship it to you.

When your order arrives, you will need to pay customs duty if applicable. Pandabuy will provide you with instructions on how to pay the customs duty. Once you have paid the customs duty, you can then enjoy your new replica clothing!
